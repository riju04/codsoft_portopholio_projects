Blog

Biking has taken me across stunning landscapes, from the winding roads of the Ladakh to the steep ascents of Saltoro Kangri. Each adventure has taught me something new, not only about biking but also about resilience, discipline, and the importance of community.


As a passionate bike rider, one of the most rewarding aspects of biking for me is the challenge of endurance rides. There’s nothing quite like the feeling of hitting the open road, knowing you have miles ahead and the freedom to ride for hours. But long-distance biking requires more than just stamina—it takes planning, mental resilience, and the right techniques.


Preparation is key for any long-distance journey. Before setting out, I always map out my route, ensuring I know where rest stops and fuel points are. Hydration is a must, so I make sure to carry enough water and energy snacks to keep me going. I also inspect my bike, checking the tires, chain, and brakes—because nothing ruins a long ride faster than unexpected mechanical problems.
